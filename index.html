How does it work?

When you select a file, it is uploaded to file.io, which will store it for 14 days. You can upload any number of files.

Then, when you select an (optional) identifier name and a place on the map for your files, you can save them on the map. Next, you only have to tell the receiving party where in the world map the files are and he can find them there.

To prevent other visitors from carelessly downloading your files, you can optionally set a password. Anything simple will prevent most people from trying to view your files. The password will be used to encrypt the file key that is given by file.io and stored in the filemap.

After 13 days your files will be erased from the filemap.
